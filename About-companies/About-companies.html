How Jobster Helps Companies

Jobster is a modern recruitment platform designed to streamline the hiring process for companies. Here's how it works:

  • Post Job Listings: Companies can easily post job openings to attract potential candidates.
  • Swipe Right Feature: Candidates browse jobs on their phone and swipe right if interested. This creates a fast, intuitive connection with potential hires.
  • Mutual Interest: When a company swipes right on a candidate's profile, a match is made.
  • Video Introduction: Upon matching, candidates are automatically prompted to record a 60-second video explaining why they are a great fit for the role.
  • Review and Proceed: Companies receive the video and can decide whether to move forward with a call screening or continue the process.
  • Streamlined Matching: Once a match is confirmed, the hiring process moves off the app, and Jobster's role is complete, leaving you to finalize the hiring.

Jobster simplifies the early stages of recruitment, saving time and allowing you to focus on finding the right talent quickly and efficiently.
